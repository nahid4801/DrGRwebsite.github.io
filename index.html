Here is my journey started!!!

I have received a Ph.D. degree in Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University, China. I am currently affiliated with the Mechanical Engineering department at Beijing University of Technology, where I teach various fluid mechanics and engineering mathematics courses and conduct research in this area. My administrative and academic career spans more than 15 years, during which I worked at institutions with high reputations both locally and internationally. My research includes the areas of Fluid dynamics, Mechanical Engineering, MHD flows, nanofluids, Theoretical Physics, Differential Equations, and Mathematical modeling of real-world problems. In addition, I am working as an academic editor and potential reviewer for several prestigious worldwide academic journals.
